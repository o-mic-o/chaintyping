Welcome to chain typing.

This is a blockchain typing game, to help improve efficiency at typing.

In order to play the game, you need to own a Chain Avatar NFT. Each Chain Avatar is represented on a Mural, of all the typing characters. There will only be 100 characters released for this v1, as generation 0. These are listed in order of most words typed correctly. They may be used as a sort of advertising space, to hold a description.

All proceeds from NFT sales go towards development of a version 2, which will be play to earn based on words typed. There is no ETA for a v2.

This game is an experiment, provides no guarentees, no liability, and no warranty at all. Chain Avatar creation are donations. They are non-refundable, and provide no guarentee of service.

Roadmap:

  • ✓ Version one - chain typing blockchain game
  • Get sufficient funding for developement of play to earn
  • Whitepaper release for play to earn game concept
  • Smart contract developement for version two
  • Improved UI for typing gameplay
