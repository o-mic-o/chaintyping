Welcome to Chain Typing

This is a blockchain typing game, to help improve efficiency at typing.

In order to play the game, it's needed to own a Chain Avatar Character. Each Chain Avatar is represented on a Mural Leaderboard, of all typing characters. There will only be 100 avatars available to play this early version of the game. These are listed in order of most words typed correctly. They may be used as a sort of advertising space, to hold a description. Donations will go to the game's DAO to fund a version 2 which will be play to earn.

This game is an experiment, provides no guarentees, no liability, and no warranty at all. Chain Avatar creation are donations. They are non-refundable, and provide no guarentee of service.
